ABOUT DARIA

        
                         
                

Daria Bardola is a seasoned fashion and commercial model with over a decade of experience in the industry. Her career spans e-commerce, editorial, and high-profile campaigns, working with globally recognized brands across the U.S. and international markets. Represented by top agencies—including Elite Miami, Elite LA, and ACE Models—Daria has built a reputation for her professionalism, versatility, and ability to maintain long-term collaborations with leading fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands. Her portfolio includes campaigns for Gelish, Merle Norman, Venus et Fleur, and Christian Cowan x Paris Hilton, along with editorial features in Cosmopolitan Ukraine, You Weekly Greece, and Cayman Vows. Whether on set for a luxury campaign or a major commercial project, Daria brings a refined presence, adaptability, and expertise that consistently set her apart. Now, as she continues to thrive in her modeling career, Daria also shares her knowledge with aspiring talent, offering insights into industry success, personal branding, and professional longevity.
